<p><strong>The Single-mode Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconical Taper (FBT) Technology</li><li>Thin-film Filter Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-mode fiber coupler market features two primary technologies: Fused Biconical Taper (FBT) and Thin-film Filter technology. FBT technology involves fusing and tapering two fiber strands to create a coupler, allowing efficient signal splitting or combining with minimal loss. In contrast, Thin-film Filter technology utilizes layers of optical coatings to selectively transmit and reflect wavelengths, enabling precise coupling for varied applications. Both methods serve critical roles in telecommunications and data transmission, enhancing signal management and network performance.</p></p>
