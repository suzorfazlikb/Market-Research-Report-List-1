<p><p>The SMD plastic film capacitor market includes three primary types based on the dielectric materials used: PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate), PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), and PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide). PEN capacitors offer excellent thermal stability and low dielectric loss, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. PET capacitors are widely used due to their balanced performance, affordability, and reliability. PPS capacitors are valued for their outstanding heat resistance and chemical stability, making them ideal for harsh environments. Each type caters to specific application needs in electronic devices.</p></p>
