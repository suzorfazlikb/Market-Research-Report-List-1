<p><p>The multi-turn valve market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on gate and globe valves. Gate valves are designed for on/off control, offering minimal flow resistance when fully open, making them ideal for applications where a straight flow path is essential. In contrast, globe valves provide finer control over flow regulation thanks to their spherical body design, allowing for precise adjustments in flow rate. Together, these valves serve crucial roles in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p></p>
