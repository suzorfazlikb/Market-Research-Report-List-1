<p><p>The Medical Device Analytical Testing Services market encompasses various testing types essential for ensuring device safety and efficacy. Material characterization assesses the physical and chemical properties of device materials. Extractable and leachable testing evaluates substances that may migrate from the device into the body. Physical testing examines the performance and durability of devices under various conditions. Sterility testing determines the absence of viable microorganisms, while bioburden testing quantifies microbial contamination, ensuring compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
