<p><ul><li>Constant Current Anemometer</li><li>Constant Pressure Anemometer</li><li>Thermostatic Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermo anemometer market can be categorized into three main types: constant current anemometers, which maintain a steady electrical current to measure airflow by temperature changes; constant pressure anemometers, which regulate pressure to detect airflow variations based on temperature shifts; and thermostatic anemometers, which utilize a temperature-sensitive element to gauge air velocity. Each type employs thermal principles for precise airflow measurement, catering to various applications in HVAC, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
