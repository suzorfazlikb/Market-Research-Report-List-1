<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Thermal Coatings market is categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower environmental impact and easier cleanup, making them ideal for eco-conscious applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings contain organic solvents, providing better adhesion and durability in certain conditions. Each type serves distinct applications, with water-based options preferred for lighter tasks, while solvent-based coatings are often used in demanding environments requiring robust performance.</p></p>
