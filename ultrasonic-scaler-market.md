<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scaler</li><li>Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scaler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic scaler market segments into two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric ultrasonic scalers. Magnetostrictive scalers operate using a magnetic field to convert electrical energy into mechanical vibrations, suitable for effective plaque removal and tooth cleaning. In contrast, piezoelectric scalers utilize a crystal mechanism that vibrates at high frequencies, offering precision and control during dental procedures. Both types enhance dental hygiene practices, catering to various preferences and requirements in dental care, demonstrating their distinct operational principles and applications.</p></p>
