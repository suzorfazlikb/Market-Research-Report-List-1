<p><p>The automotive real-time clocks (RTCs) market encompasses various types, including I2C-bus RTCs, which utilize the I2C interface for efficient communication and control. SPI RTCs employ the Serial Peripheral Interface for faster data transfer rates. Integrated quartz crystal RTCs combine the RTC and crystal into a single unit, enhancing accuracy and reliability. Ultra-low-power RTCs are optimized for minimal energy consumption to extend battery life in automotive applications. Each type serves specific requirements in vehicle timing and synchronization.</p></p>
