<p><p>The IV tubing sets and accessories market encompasses three primary types: Peripheral Intravenous Catheter Insertion, Central Venous Catheter Placement, and PICC Line Insertion. Peripheral IV catheters are short-term solutions for administering medications and fluids, while Central Venous Catheters provide long-term access for patients requiring extensive treatment. PICC lines combine the benefits of both, allowing for prolonged intravenous access through a peripheral insertion. Each type addresses specific clinical needs and patient conditions within the broader intravenous therapy landscape.</p></p>
