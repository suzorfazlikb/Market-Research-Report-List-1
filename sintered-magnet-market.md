<p><ul><li>Sintered Rare Earth Magnets</li><li>Sintered Ferrite Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sintered magnet market comprises primarily two types: sintered rare earth magnets and sintered ferrite magnets. Sintered rare earth magnets, made from alloys like neodymium and samarium, offer high magnetic strength and are widely used in applications requiring compact solutions, such as electric motors and electronics. In contrast, sintered ferrite magnets, composed of iron oxide mixed with barium or strontium, are cost-effective and corrosion-resistant, making them suitable for applications like household appliances and toys. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
