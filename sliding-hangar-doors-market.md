<p><p>Sliding hangar doors are classified into two primary market types: with tailgate and without tailgate configurations. Doors with tailgates feature an additional section that can be opened independently, facilitating easier access for smaller vehicles and equipment without fully opening the entire door. In contrast, doors without tailgates operate as single, large panels, offering unobstructed entry but requiring full clearance. Both types cater to various operational needs in aviation, storage, and industrial applications, enhancing functionality and accessibility.</p></p>
