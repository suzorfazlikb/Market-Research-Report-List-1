<p><p>License Plate Capture (LPC) cameras are categorized into three main types: mobile, fixed, and portable. Mobile LPC cameras are mounted on vehicles, allowing for flexible monitoring of traffic and real-time plate recognition. Fixed LPC cameras are stationary and installed at strategic locations like parking lots and toll booths to capture plates consistently. Portable LPC cameras are compact and easy to transport, enabling temporary deployment for specific events or locations. Each type serves distinct needs in vehicle tracking and security applications.</p></p>
