<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Multipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rechargeable implantable pulse generator market is categorized into unipolar and multipolar configurations. Unipolar pulse generators utilize a single electrode for stimulation, providing a simpler design and potentially reducing complexity in implantation. In contrast, multipolar pulse generators involve multiple electrodes, allowing for more targeted stimulation and versatile therapeutic applications. This differentiation in design affects the efficacy, patient outcomes, and the development of new technologies within the market, catering to varying medical needs and enhancing treatment options.</p></p>
