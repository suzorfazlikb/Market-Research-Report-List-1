<p><p>The laser cancer therapy market comprises various types, including Carbon Dioxide (CO2) lasers, Argon lasers, and Yttrium-Aluminum-Garnet (Nd:YAG) lasers. CO2 lasers excel in ablation and vaporization of soft tissue, making them effective for surface tumors. Argon lasers are utilized primarily for their precision in treating specific tissues, particularly in ophthalmology. Nd:YAG lasers penetrate deeper tissues, allowing for treatment of more aggressive cancers and larger tumors. These technologies enhance treatment efficacy while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissues.</p></p>
