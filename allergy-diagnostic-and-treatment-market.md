<p><strong>The Allergy Diagnostic and Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In Vivo Tests</li><li>In Vitro Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The allergy diagnostic and treatment market comprises two primary types: in vivo tests and in vitro tests. In vivo tests involve direct exposure to allergens, typically through skin prick or patch tests, allowing for immediate observation of allergic reactions. In vitro tests, on the other hand, analyze blood samples for the presence of specific antibodies or immune responses to allergens, providing a laboratory-based assessment. Both approaches are essential for diagnosing allergies and guiding appropriate treatment strategies.</p></p>
