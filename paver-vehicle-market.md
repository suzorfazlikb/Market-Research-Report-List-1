<p><p>The paver market for vehicles is primarily divided into two significant types: mechanical pavers and hydrostatic pavers. Mechanical pavers utilize a traditional system where the forward motion is generated through mechanical gears and chains, offering durability and simplicity. In contrast, hydrostatic pavers employ a hydraulic system for smoother operation and enhanced maneuverability, providing better control on various terrains. Both types are essential for efficient road construction, each catering to specific project needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
