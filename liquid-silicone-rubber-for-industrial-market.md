<p><p>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) is a versatile material widely used in the industrial sector, available mainly in two types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component liquid rubber is pre-cured, requiring no mixing, making it easy to handle and ideal for automated processes. In contrast, two-component liquid rubber involves mixing a catalyst with the base material, allowing for greater customization and curing flexibility. Both types offer excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and durability, making them suitable for various applications, including automotive, medical, and consumer goods.</p></p>
