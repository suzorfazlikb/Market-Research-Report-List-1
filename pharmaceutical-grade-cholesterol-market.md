<p><p>The pharmaceutical-grade cholesterol market is primarily categorized into NF (National Formulary) Grade and BP (British Pharmacopoeia) Grade. NF Grade cholesterol meets specific purity standards set for use in pharmaceuticals and dietary supplements within the United States. Conversely, BP Grade cholesterol adheres to the stringent quality requirements established by the British Pharmacopoeia, ensuring its suitability for medical applications in the UK and globally. Both grades are essential for formulating products that require high-quality cholesterol for therapeutic and research purposes.</p></p>
