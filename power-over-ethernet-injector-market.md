<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) injector market is categorized primarily into two types: single-port midspan and multi-port midspan. Single-port midspans are designed to add power to a single Ethernet connection, making them ideal for specific use cases like powering individual devices in remote locations. On the other hand, multi-port midspans can supply power to multiple devices simultaneously, suited for installations with several PoE-enabled devices. Both types enhance network flexibility and simplify device management in various applications.</p></p>
