<p><p>Lawyer liability insurance encompasses various market types, primarily Directors and Officers (D&O) Insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) Insurance. D&O Insurance protects legal professionals against claims arising from decisions made in their managerial roles, covering defense costs and potential settlements. E&O Insurance safeguards against claims related to negligence, mistakes, or inadequate work in providing legal services, ensuring that lawyers can manage the financial repercussions of their professional activities. Both types are essential for risk management in the legal field.</p></p>
